Who is Hermes Rocket???????

he Hermes Rocket is a vintage portable typewriter manufactured by the Swiss company E. Paillard & Cie, which later became known as Hermes. The Hermes Rocket typewriter was first introduced in the 1930s and gained popularity for its compact and lightweight design, making it a popular choice for writers on the go. It features a distinctive and sleek appearance, often characterized by its rounded keys and compact case.
